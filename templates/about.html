About Us

Yonse news is a local news aggregation service that aims to provide the Zambian landscape with a central news hub of local news articles, collected from various Zambian owned news sources.

Mission

To make local news, drawn from various sources, easily accessible for every Zambian wherever they may be, in a centralized space.

How it works

Yonse news articles are shown for not more than 36 hours, after which they can no longer be found on our platform. This is to ensure that we keep our users up-to-date with the lastest local news, and also to allow the users to visit the source sites if they are interested in similar articles.
